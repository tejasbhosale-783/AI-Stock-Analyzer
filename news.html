GOLD PRICE HIKE


Gold's relationship with the stock market is complex and can be both positive and negative, but it generally tends to be a safe-haven asset during periods of market stress, often showing an inverse correlation. This means that when the stock market declines, gold prices tend to rise, and vice versa. However, this relationship isn't always consistent and can change depending on the specific circumstances.
